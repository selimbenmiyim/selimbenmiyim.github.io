Privacy Policy

What we collect

Lattem does not collect any personal information such as your name, email address, location, etc. We only save minor settings, such as theme preferences, locally on your device. None of your data is sent to our server. Some external services (such as fonts or icon libraries) may receive standard technical request data automatically.

Children

Lattem is designed for all ages, but parents should supervise their children's use of the app. Like all apps, it's important to ensure children are using the app safely and appropriately.

Third parties

We do not sell or share your data with third parties. Some fonts or icons may be loaded from external services for appearance.

Your choices

You can clear stored browser data at any time to remove local preferences and game progress.

Contact

  • Lattem is developed by a solo developer who is not affiliated with any company.
  • No public contact information is currently available.
